About Us

JP-Technology is a leading Information Technology (IT) firm dedicated to training aspiring software developers in the art of building responsive and interactive websites. Our comprehensive programs cover both front-end and back-end web development, equipping learners with the essential skills needed to excel in the modern tech industry. On the front-end, we provide hands-on training in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React, and Python, enabling our students to design dynamic and user-friendly interfaces. For the back-end, we offer in-depth instruction in MySQL, PHP, and MongoDB, ensuring a strong foundation in server-side programming and database management. At JP-Technology, we are committed to empowering individuals with practical, industry-relevant skills that prepare them for successful careers in web development.
